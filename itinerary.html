Free & Easy Afternoon

Enjoy your free & easy time at Sydney, discover this place in different perspective is the best activities to recharge. Take a stroll through the Queen Victoria Building or wander around Wooloomooloo and the Sydney Opera House or do a bridge climb on the Sydney Harbour Bridge. Other local favorite sights include SEA Life Aquarium, Taronga Zoo, Darling Harbour, Manly and Bondi Beach. Sydney is an easy city to get around, with the metropolitan area servicing a great network of public transport, roadways and walking paths.
