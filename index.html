Introduction to Sydney

Sydney, capital of New South Wales and one of Australia's largest cities, is best known for its harbourfront Sydney Opera House, with a distinctive sail-like design. Massive Darling Harbour and the smaller Circular Quay port are hubs of waterside life, with the arched Harbour Bridge and esteemed Royal Botanic Garden nearby. Enjoy Sydney’s natural beauty, from beaches to public gardens and, of course, the sparkling harbour, before discovering its thriving restaurants and markets.

Link to travel guide of Sydney: https://www.sydney.com/
